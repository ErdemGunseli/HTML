La Tour Eiffel

Paris, France

Constructed 1887-1889

Learn about the construction of the Eiffel Tower!

The Construction Schedule

  1. The first floor was completed on the 1st of April 1888.
  2. The second floor was completed on the 14th of August 1888.
  3. The top section was completed on the 31st of March 1888.

Facts and Figures

  • The tower has 1665 steps.
  • 2.5 Million rivers were used.
  • 7,300 tonnes of iron and 60 tonnes of paint was used.
